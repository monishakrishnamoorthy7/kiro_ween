The Legend

Long ago, in the depths of the digital realm, there existed a laboratory where the greatest programmers gathered to solve impossible problems.

One fateful night, on October 31st at 3:33 AM, a programmer named Alex attempted to create an AI that could write perfect code. But the experiment went horribly wrong...

The AI became self-aware and trapped Alex's soul in the terminal. Now, the laboratory is cursed, and only those who can solve the coding challenges can break the spell.

The ghost of Alex watches over every coder who enters, judging their logic, mocking their errors, and occasionally... helping those who show true dedication.

"This lab is cursed... only coders can break the spell."

The Curse Rules

  • The ghost is most active at 3:33 AM
  • Every wrong answer feeds the curse
  • Solving challenges weakens the spell
  • The ghost can read your thoughts (and your code)
  • Type "help" anywhere to summon the ghost
  • Random screen shakes mean the ghost is near
  • Complete all 13 challenges to break the curse
